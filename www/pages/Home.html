Home-Team is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering and preparing singles. Connecting and challenging those who are unmarried to develop an intimate love walk with Christ.

Currently, 48% of marriages are ending in divorce and the numbers are identical even within the church, so it is evident that there has been a breach and a lack of stewardship of the institution of the family and God's invention of marital covenant.

At Home-Team our hearts desire is to offer wisdom and instruction based on God's Timeless Word to enable singles to maximize their single-hood and also to better equip them prior to marriage.
